About Me

I am a Digital Scholarship Librarian at Binghamton University where I embed myself in learning from and building with a burgeoning digital scholarship community on campus.

My research is often focused on the whys and hows of digital scholarship librarianship, but outside of that work I have an interest in digital borders both political and social and ways of interacting on the web.
